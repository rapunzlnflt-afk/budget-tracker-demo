Savings Goals

Your Goals

Pay Yourself First

Treat savings like a bill. Transfer money to your goal the day you get paid, before spending.

Start Small

Even $25/week adds up to $1,300/year. Consistency beats big one-time deposits.

Visualize Progress

Watching your progress bar grow is motivating. Check in weekly to stay on track.

Extra Payments

Avoid New Debt

While paying off debt, freeze credit card spending. Use cash or debit to avoid backsliding.
